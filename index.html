Concept and Vision

Creating a smart, safe, and economical system for car sharing that minimizes idle car time and promotes sustainability on campus.

Economical

Owners earn income; renters save costs. All prices are clearly listed in Omani Rial (OMR).

Safe & Verified

Every user verifies their identity and license. Every transaction is protected.

Sustainable Campus

Reduce car idling time and promote efficient resource use among students.

Core Platform Features

All the tools needed for smooth and reliable sharing.

Role-Based System

  • Sign up as Car Owner or Renter.
  • Password rule: minimum 6 characters.
  • Single Session Rule enforced for security.

Owner Management Tools

  • Create and Edit detailed car listings.
  • Manage availability time slots (e.g., 9:00 a.m. to 11:00 a.m.).
  • Monitor booking requests and approve/reject them.

Renter Booking Process

  • View available cars immediately after login.
  • Secure payment via manual transfer to (+968 76944031).
  • Mandatory upload of payment receipt for confirmation.

Communication and Trust

  • In-App Messaging system for owner/renter communication.
  • Post-rental rating system for mutual trust.
  • Revenue split: 50% to Owner, 50% to Platform.
